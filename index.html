How it works

Three steps, designed to fit exploration workflows.

  1. 1

    Deploy detectors

    Install modular muon detectors at selected positions (surface or shallow underground) based on site constraints.

  2. 2

    Collect muon tracks

    Cosmic muons pass through the target volume. Track statistics accumulate over time, naturally.

  3. 3

    Reconstruct & interpret

    We reconstruct density-related observables and provide maps + uncertainty, ready to feed into your modeling loop.
